Destinations

Carrascal

Carrascal is a coastal town with pristine beaches, a variety of marine life, as well as lively festivals and a long history.

Cantilan

The natural wonders, extensive history, active culture, and stunning beaches of Cantilan make it a place worth exploring.

Madrid

Madrid is a town with lovely  picturesque vistas, a relaxed way of life, welcoming residents, and a feeling of a fishing community.

Carmen

Carmen has a long history and a wide range of tourist attractions, such as caves, waterfalls, beaches, and annual festivals.

Lanuza

Lanuza is a coastal community renowned for its world-class surfing spot, calm beaches, pristine reefs, and beautiful surroundings.
